# 🎵 Music Folder

Place your background music here!

## What Goes Here

**romantic.mp3** - Soft romantic background music (optional)
- Format: MP3
- Duration: 2-4 minutes
- Recommended size: < 5MB
- Volume: Will play at 30% (auto-set)

## Optional Setup

To enable background music:

1. Place your music file here as: **romantic.mp3**
2. Edit `script.js` around line 207
3. Uncomment/add this line:
   ```javascript
   backgroundMusic.src = 'assets/music/romantic.mp3';
   ```
