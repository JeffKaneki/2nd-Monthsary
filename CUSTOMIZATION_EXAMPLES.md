<div class="letter-text" id="letterText">
My Dearest Love,

Where do I even begin? These two months with you have been the most beautiful chapter of my life.

Before you, I didn't know what it meant to truly love someone. I didn't know that my heart could feel so full, so safe, so completely at home.

Every morning I wake up grateful. Grateful for your smile, your laugh, your presence. Grateful that you chose me.

You've shown me that love isn't just about the big romantic moments—though those are beautiful. It's about the little things: the way you squeeze my hand, how you listen to every word I say, the way you make me feel safe.

With you, I found my person. My best friend. My home.

I promise to love you fiercely, support your dreams, and make you smile every single day.

Forever yours,
